📖 Biography

Hollywood's go-to voice actor. Voiced characters in nearly every major Disney animated film. His Heihei in Moana became a fan favorite. Also played Wash in Fire

🎬 Career Story

Alan studied at Juilliard. His iconic role was Hoban 'Wash' Washburne in Firefly (2002) and Serenity (2005) -- one of sci-fi's most beloved characters. His Disney voice roles include: King Candy (Wreck-It Ralph), Duke of Weaselton (Frozen), Duke Weaselton (Zootopia), KnowsMore (Ralph Breaks the Internet), Iago (Aladdin), Terry (Soul), and Heihei (Moana). The 'Alan Tudyk Disney Curse' -- he always voices characters who turn out to be secretly villainous -- became a running joke. Heihei was the hilarious exception.
