🎬 Career Story

Born into a family of professional wrestlers, Dwayne first rose to fame in the WWE as 'The Rock,' becoming a 10-time WWE Champion. His film debut came in The Mummy Returns (2001) as the Scorpion King, earning a record $5.5 million salary for a first-time lead. His blockbusters include Fast and Furious, Jumanji, San Andreas, Central Intelligence, Rampage, Skyscraper, Red Notice, and Black Adam. He founded Seven Bucks Productions, launched Teremana Tequila, and has been named one of Time's 100 Most Influential People multiple times.
