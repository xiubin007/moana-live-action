📖 Biography

Legendary New Zealand actor with a 4-decade career. Played Jango Fett in Star Wars, Boba Fett in The Book of Boba Fett, and Aquaman's father in both Aquaman fil

🎬 Career Story

His career-defining role was Jake 'The Muss' Heke in Once Were Warriors (1994), a cultural landmark in New Zealand cinema. George Lucas cast him as Jango Fett in Star Wars: Episode II (2002). He returned as Boba Fett in The Book of Boba Fett (2021). He also played Thomas Curry in Aquaman (2018) and its sequel.
